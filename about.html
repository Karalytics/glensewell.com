About Glen Sewell

With more than 20 years of experience in data management, analytics, governance, risk, and enterprise transformation, I help organizations establish trusted data foundations, strengthen governance, and operationalize responsible AI in complex and regulated environments.

Having spent the first half of my career in analytics, customer intelligence, and business decision support, I bring a business-first perspective to governance. I understand how organizations create value from data and how governance enables that value to be delivered responsibly. This perspective allows me to bridge the gap between business objectives, governance requirements, and regulatory expectations.

My Background

My career began in analytics, customer intelligence, CRM, and decision support where success was measured by business outcomes. I worked closely with business leaders to develop customer strategies, improve performance, and deliver measurable results through data-driven decision making.

As organizations became increasingly dependent on data, I expanded my focus into enterprise data management, governance, risk, compliance, and operating model design. Today, I help organizations create trusted data foundations that enable both innovation and effective risk management.

More recently, my work has evolved to include responsible AI, model governance, regulatory compliance, records management, and third-party risk management. I bring practical experience helping organizations navigate emerging AI requirements while maintaining a focus on business value.
